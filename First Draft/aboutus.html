About Us 💛

Sahaयात्री is a community-driven digital platform dedicated to making professional home-care accessible and affordable across all 77 districts of Nepal. We bridge the gap between households and qualified healthcare providers, including nurses, nursing students, and home-care assistants. Unlike traditional agencies, we prioritize "Time Flexibility," allowing families to book help for specific durations like 3-hour, 6-hour, or full-time shifts based on their actual needs.

By eliminating middleman fees and providing a direct directory of verified professionals, Sahaयात्री empowers healthcare workers with flexible opportunities while ensuring every family receives the local, high-quality medical support they deserve.
